About Me

Greetings! I’m Pramit Dutta, a Master of Applied Science (MASc) candidate in Computer Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence at the University of Guelph. I have a bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the AI enabled Medical Imaging Lab under the supervision of Dr. Eranga Ukwatta.

My research primarily focuses on multimodal approaches, exploring how different types of data, can be integrated to improve model performance. I am particularly interested in utilizing self-supervised learning (SSL) and Vision-Language Models (VLM) to develop data-efficient and scalable solutions. My goal is to advance AI-driven healthcare solutions by integrating techniques like self-supervised learning with Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and RL within multimodal frameworks.
